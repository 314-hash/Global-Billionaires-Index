## Utility

### 1. Governance
- 1 MERG = 1 Vote.
- Minimum quorum required for proposal execution.

### 2. Staking
- **APY**: Dynamic based on TVL.
- **Lock-up**: Optional lock-up periods for multiplier rewards (e.g., 2x for 1 year lock).

### 3. Deflationary Mechanisms
- **Buyback & Burn**: Protocol revenue is used to buy back MERG from the open market and burn it, reducing supply over time.
- **Penalty Burns**: Early unstaking penalties are burned.
